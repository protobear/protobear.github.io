404

Once upon a midnight dreary

While I web surfed, weak and weary,
For pages long forgotten yore.
When I clicked my fav'rite href,
Suddenly there came a warning, and my heart was filled with mourning,
Mourning for my dear /missing.pdf,
"Tis not possible!", I muttered, "Give thine pages, I emplore!"
Quoth the server, 404.

Marnick De Grave
