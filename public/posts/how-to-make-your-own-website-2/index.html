How to make your own website

2024-12-08

One of my favorite writers, Gita Jackson, wrote an article for Aftermath (One of my favorite websites) titled “For The Love of God, Make Your Own Website”. It’s a good article, go read it.

So in doing my part of making the internet a better place, and also because I’m an educator, I’m writing a series of articles about self-hosting your own website.  Why? Because for the internet to stay weird, interesting, and creative, people need the freedom to experiment, push boundaries, and discover limitations, all without being bogged down by ongoing costs or platform restrictions.

Let’s take this journey together. We’ll cover the essentials: how to get a server, set it up, build your website, acquire and configure your domain, and finally, serve your site to the world. Let’s reclaim a little bit of the internet’s magic.
